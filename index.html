Welcome to the site! This is the home page for the Unconfined payment processor. The Unconfined payment processor aims to make it easy to accept Ethereum (and more currencies in the future) payments for services or products. The service is easy to use and can be integrated into almost any pre-existing language very easily, so no matter how your website is designed, adapting it to accept crypto payments is simple and rapid.


The service is free during early development - when an early version of the final product is complete, a one time charge will be required for continued use. Unconfined doesn't and won't charge a fee on top of what the user pays for the product; once you have a valid license to use the service you'll never have to pay again.
